# Privacy Policy

**Electrician Pro X**

**Last Updated: November 29, 2025**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Electrician Pro X ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our mobile application Electrician Pro X (the "App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address
- Name (optional)
- Password (encrypted)

If you choose to sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID credentials and any information you authorize Apple to share (such as your name and email address). Apple may provide us with a private relay email address if you choose to hide your email.

### Job and Business Data
When you use the App to manage your electrical business, you may enter:
- Job names and descriptions
- Client names and contact information (including email addresses)
- Job locations and addresses
- Start dates and times
- Job costs and pricing
- Job photos
- Estimates and invoices

This information is stored to provide you with the App's job management, invoicing, and estimating features.

### Automatically Collected Information
We may automatically collect certain information when you use the App, including:
- Device type and operating system
- App usage statistics
- Crash reports and performance data

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Create and manage your account
- Provide job management, invoicing, and estimating features
- Send estimates and invoices to your clients on your behalf (when you request)
- Process subscription payments
- Send you important updates about the App or your account
- Improve and optimize the App
- Respond to customer support requests

## How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following situations:

### Service Providers
We use third-party services to operate the App:
- **Firebase (Google)**: For authentication, database storage, and analytics
- **Resend**: For sending emails (estimates and invoices) on your behalf
- **RevenueCat**: For managing subscriptions and payments
- **Apple**: For processing App Store subscriptions and Sign in with Apple

### Client Communications
When you send an estimate or invoice through the App, the recipient's email address and the document contents are shared with our email service provider (Resend) to deliver the email.

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

## Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase, a Google Cloud service. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention

We retain your account information and job data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Update
You can access and update your account information within the App at any time.

### Delete Your Account
You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at support@proxtrades.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

### Email Communications
You can opt out of promotional emails by following the unsubscribe instructions in those emails. You will still receive transactional emails related to your account and services.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:
