Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. If you'd like to delete your Electrician Pro X account and all associated data, you can do so directly within the app or by contacting us.

Delete Account In-App

  1. Open the Electrician Pro X app on your device
  2. Go to the Account tab
  3. Scroll down and tap the Delete Account button (red button)
  4. Confirm your decision when prompted

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account profile and login credentials
  • All jobs, estimates, and invoices you've created
  • Photos and documents attached to your jobs
  • Time tracking records and analytics data
  • Team associations and job invitations

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please make sure to export any data you need before deleting your account.

Need Help?

If you're unable to access the app or need assistance with account deletion, please contact us:

Email: support@proxtrades.com

Please include the email address associated with your account in your request.

We typically process deletion requests within 48 hours of receiving them.
